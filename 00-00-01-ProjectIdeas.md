# ⭐ Tips to Keep Them “Medium Difficulty”

To maintain medium complexity:

* Use **Class-Based Views**
* Keep UI simple (Bootstrap or custom CSS)
* Use **AJAX for live updates** (like likes, search, drag-drop)
* Use **SQLite** to keep database simple
* Avoid heavy third-party services unless needed
